Welcome To Learning Lions Bootcamp Cohort 4


"This is Bootcamp 4 by Learning Lions, following the Mobile LEAP training, marking its second phase. Spanning 8 weeks, it offers a more rigorous curriculum than Mobile LEAP, inviting only 20 top-performing students from that program based solely on academic merit. Participants specialize in either Computer Creative or Tech, honing their skills deeply within their chosen field. The program also includes training in freelancing and essential soft skills crucial for freelance success. Graduates of this phase are eligible for the Advanced Track, the final phase of our training program. Our current cohort includes students from Mobile LEAP 5 and 6."
